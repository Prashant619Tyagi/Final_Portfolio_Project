About Minor Project.

In my project on the implementation of the Sinkhole Attack, I successfully designed and executed a comprehensive simulation of a real-world cyberattack scenario. Using the powerful combination of Visual Studio Community and the NETSIM Simulator, I aimed to understand and analyze the potential vulnerabilities and impacts of this type of attack on a network.
Developed a sophisticated sinkhole attack project, utilizing NETSIM simulation and incorporating malware techniques to redirect network traffic to four intruders within the server. Conducted extensive analysis to assess the attack's impact on the network and individual nodes.
Utilized a variety of metrics such as throughput, jitters, and other relevant indicators to evaluate the attack's effects.
Leveraged the collected data and analysis to optimize the attack, achieving a notable improvement of 5% in terms of performance.
This project enabled me to gain valuable insights into the intricacies of sinkhole attacks and their potential consequences for network security. By simulating real-world scenarios and analyzing the impact on network performance, I enhanced my understanding of countermeasures and optimization strategies to mitigate such threats effectively.
